How its work

The process of donating blood is simple and safe..

1. Register

Create Your donnor account and complete your profile with nessary details.

2. Schedule

Book an Appointment at your nearest donation center at your convenience.

3.Donate

Visit the center, complete a quick health check, and donate blood to save lives.

Why Donate Blood?

  • Save lives

    • A single donation can save up to 3 lives.

  • Health Benefits

    • Regular donation helps maintain healthy iron levels.

  • Free Health Check

    • Receive a free mini health screening with every donation

Are you Eligible?

  • Age: 18-65 years old.
  • Weight: At least 50kg (110 lbs)
  • Good Health Condition
  • No tattoos/piercings in last 6 months
  • NO major surgery in last 6 months
  • NOt pregnant or breastfeeding
