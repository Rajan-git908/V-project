Are you Eligible?

  • Age: 18-65 years old.
  • Weight: At least 50kg (110 lbs)
  • Good Health Condition
  • No tattoos/piercings in last 6 months
  • NO major surgery in last 6 months
  • NOt pregnant or breastfeeding
